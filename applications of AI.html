Applications of AI

Artificial Intelligence in the shape of neural networks and expert systems has applications in almost all human activities.

The combination of high precision and low computation time makes AI a slicing part technology.

Robot ES‟s are already taking over workshop level jobs in large industries, for this reason facet lining people into a extra supervisory role. Stock brokerage firms are now the use of Artificial Intelligence to analyze data, make analysis and purchase or promote shares without the interference of any people.

Some of the applications of Artificial Intelligence are gaming industry, heavy industries, climate forecasting, experts systems and data mining or understanding extraction.
