Definition of AI

Artificial Intelligence (abbreviated as AI) is the functionality of a device to carry out activities, which could otherwise handiest be anticipated of the human brain. These activities include the ability for understanding and the capability to acquire it. It also incorporates of the capacity to judge, apprehend relationships and last but not least produce original thoughts . From SIRI to self-driving cars, artificial intelligence (AI) is progressing rapidly. While technology fiction frequently portrays AI as robots with human-like characteristics, AI can encompass something from Google’s search algorithms to IBM’s Watson to autonomous guns.
